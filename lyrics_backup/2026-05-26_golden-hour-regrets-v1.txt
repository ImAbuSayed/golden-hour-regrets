[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The sun is sinking low across the wooden floor
I am holding on but wanting something more
Fire light is dancing deep inside your eyes
We are losing track of time in sweet replies

[Verse 1]
Shadows stretching long across this sleepy town
Driving empty roads but I am turning round
I can feel your warmth still lingering on my skin
Dreaming of the places that we could have been

[Pre-Chorus]
Every passing moment is pulling me near
Softly breathing secrets I just want to hear
The midnight closing in and the room is so still
If I do not make a move I know the silence will

[Chorus]
These golden hour regrets are heavy on my mind
Leaving every piece of my history behind
I want to pull you close and never let you go
Feeling every heartbeat steady and slow

[Post-Chorus]
Steady and slow, burning through the night
Keeping you close until the morning light

[Verse 2]
Rain is falling soft upon the window glass
Watching every second of the evening pass
I am tracing every line upon your gentle face
Getting lost forever in this quiet space

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]
These golden hour regrets are heavy on my mind
Leaving every piece of my history behind

Feeling every heartbeat steady and slow

[Post-Chorus 2]
Steady and slow, burning through the night
Keeping you close until the morning light

[Bridge]
These golden hour regrets are heavy on my mind
I am searching for the words I just can never find
The rhythm of this love is calling out so clear
Just listen as Saeed is whispering in your ear
So let the music fade and let the candles burn
This is the only lesson I will ever learn

[Spoken]
I just want to hold you in the dark
Let this midnight leave a lasting mark

[Final Chorus]
These golden hour regrets are heavy on my mind
